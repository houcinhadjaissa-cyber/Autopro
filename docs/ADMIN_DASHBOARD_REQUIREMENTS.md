# Admin Dashboard Requirements

## 1. UI/UX Strategy: "The Facebook Standard"
*   **Fully Responsive:** The dashboard must be "Platform Aware." It must look like a native mobile app on a phone and a professional management suite on a desktop.
*   **Urgent Actions:** Key management tools (Approve Payout, Block User, Emergency Stop) must be easily accessible on mobile views without feeling "bulky" or "weird."

## 2. Global Intelligence & Search
*   **Omni-Search:** A global search bar at the top of every page.
*   **Instant Results:** Admins must be able to find any record (Order ID, VIN, Phone Number, User Name, Part Number) instantly from one search box.

## 3. Business Verification & Onboarding
*   **Automated Entry:** New shops and suppliers can sign up and go live automatically to ensure platform growth.
*   **Trust Tiers:** License uploads are not mandatory for entry but are required to unlock "Verified" badges, higher visibility, and increased customer trust.

## 4. Operational Controls
*   **Maintenance Mode:** A "One-Click Maintenance" system. The owner can put a specific country (e.g., just Algeria) or the entire global platform on hold during technical issues or emergencies.
*   **Urgent Alerts:** Real-time notifications for high-priority events, such as high-value orders, large refund requests, or security flags.

## 5. Multi-Project Management
*   **Project Toggle:** Using the "Reusable Core," the dashboard must include a simple project switcher.
*   **Unified Control:** Switch between managing Autopro and any future owner projects (Grocery, Medical, etc.) from the same admin interface without logging out.

## 6. Financial & Staff Oversight
*   **Money Flow:** Mobile-optimized views for approving payouts and managing business debt.
*   **Staff Audit:** Track staff performance and activity logs directly from the mobile-optimized dashboard.
