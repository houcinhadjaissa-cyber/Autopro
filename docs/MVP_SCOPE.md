# Autopro MVP Scope (Version 1.0)

## 1. Primary Launch Targets
*   **Countries:** Algeria and Nigeria.
*   **Goal:** Establish a cross-border automotive marketplace.

## 2. Core User Experience (Day One)
*   **Full Marketplace:** Autopro must launch with Clients, Suppliers (Parts), and Service Providers (Garages) active. This is the foundation of the platform.
*   **TecDoc Integration:** The system must be ready to connect to TecDoc API. Initial data will be filled by invited suppliers and shops.

## 3. Financial & Payment Setup
*   **Mandatory Methods:** Cash on Delivery (COD) and Online Card payments.
*   **Flexibility:** The structure must allow Bank Transfer and other methods to be toggled "ON" easily without new code.

## 4. Social Commerce (TikTok-Style)
*   **Ready on Day One:** The video infrastructure must be installed from the start. 
*   **Strategy:** Launch with demonstration videos for top-selling products to keep initial server hosting costs manageable.

## 5. Launch Strategy
*   **Phase 1 (Soft Launch):** Invite-only for selected shops and suppliers.
*   **Phase 2 (Stress Test):** Verify system stability under load.
*   **Phase 3 (Public):** Open registration once the TecDoc data and core supply chain are verified.
